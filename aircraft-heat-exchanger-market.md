<p><strong>The Aircraft Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate-fin</li><li>Flat Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft heat exchanger market comprises two primary types: plate-fin and flat tube designs. Plate-fin heat exchangers utilize a series of plates with fins to maximize surface area, enhancing heat transfer efficiency in compact spaces, ideal for lightweight aircraft. In contrast, flat tube heat exchangers employ flat tubes to facilitate efficient heat exchange while minimizing weight and resistance to airflow. Both types are crucial for managing temperatures in aircraft systems, ensuring optimal performance and safety in various operational conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>Industrial radiography is a non-destructive testing technique used for examining the integrity of materials through imaging. The market is divided into two main types: digital and film-based. Digital radiography utilizes advanced sensors and imaging software to produce instant, high-quality images, allowing for real-time analysis and improved efficiency. In contrast, film-based radiography relies on traditional photographic film to capture images, which can be time-consuming and less environmentally friendly. Both methods are crucial for quality assurance in industries like construction, manufacturing, and aerospace.</p></p>

The P-anisic acid market is characterized by its widespread use in fragrances and as a flavoring agent, while M-anisic acid finds niche applications in pharmaceuticals and agrochemicals. O-anisic acid, although less common, is utilized in specialty chemicals and research settings, reflecting diverse market demands across sectors.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Low Iron Flat Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low iron flat glass market is primarily categorized into rolled glass and float glass types. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a textured surface, suitable for applications like decorative windows. Float glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth and clear surface, ideal for high-visibility applications such as architectural glazing. Both types are essential in industries that require enhanced clarity and reduced iron content for superior optical quality.</p></p>

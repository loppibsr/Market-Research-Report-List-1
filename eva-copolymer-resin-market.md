<p><p>EVA Copolymer Resin is categorized based on vinyl acetate (VA) content, influencing its properties and applications. Resins with **<18% VA** are more rigid, offering higher tensile strength and better chemical resistance, making them suitable for films, foams, and adhesives requiring durability. Resins with **≥18% VA** are softer, more flexible, and exhibit enhanced elasticity, ideal for applications like footwear, flexible packaging, and sealants. Both types cater to diverse industries, with VA content determining their mechanical and thermal characteristics for specific end-use requirements.</p></p>

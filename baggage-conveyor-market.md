<p><p>The baggage conveyor market consists of two primary types: conveyor belts and conveyor rollers. Conveyor belts are continuous loops that transport luggage efficiently in airports, providing a smooth surface for various bag sizes. Conveyor rollers, on the other hand, utilize cylindrical rollers to facilitate luggage movement, often seen in sorting systems. Both types are essential for enhancing operational efficiency, speeding up baggage handling processes, and improving overall airport logistics, ensuring that baggage reaches its destination swiftly and safely.</p></p>

Furnace Black, the most common, is produced using oil or gas and offers high performance in tires and rubber. Channel Black provides superior balance and lower viscosity, ideal for specialty applications. Thermal Black, created through pyrolysis, offers high purity and is used in coatings and plastics. Acetylene Black is premium, with high conductivity, suitable for batteries and electronics. Other types cater to niche market needs.</p></p>

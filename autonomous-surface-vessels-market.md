<p><p>The Autonomous Surface Vessels (ASV) market is primarily divided into two categories: surface and subsurface markets. Surface ASVs operate on the water's surface, performing tasks such as data collection, surveillance, and transport. They are typically used for marine research, defense, and commercial shipping. On the other hand, subsurface vessels, often referred to as autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs), function below the water's surface, specializing in underwater exploration, environmental monitoring, and underwater inspections. Both markets focus on enhancing operational efficiency and reducing human intervention.</p></p>

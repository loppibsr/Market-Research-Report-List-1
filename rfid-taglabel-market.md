<p><strong>The RFID Tag/Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tag/label market is divided into two main types: active and passive. Active RFID tags contain their own power source, allowing for longer read ranges and real-time tracking, making them suitable for applications like asset management and logistics. Passive RFID tags, on the other hand, draw energy from the RFID reader, making them cost-effective and ideal for high-volume applications such as inventory tracking and access control. Each type serves different needs based on range, cost, and application.</p></p>

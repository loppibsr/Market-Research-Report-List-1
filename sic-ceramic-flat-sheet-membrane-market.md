<p><p>The SiC ceramic flat sheet membrane market is categorized into microfiltration and ultrafiltration types, each serving distinct applications. Microfiltration membranes operate at larger pore sizes, effectively removing suspended solids, bacteria, and larger molecules, making them ideal for water treatment and food processing. Ultrafiltration membranes, with smaller pore sizes, provide enhanced separation capabilities, targeting smaller particles, colloids, and macromolecules. These membranes are widely used in industries like pharmaceuticals and wastewater treatment, leveraging their durability and resistance to fouling.</p></p>

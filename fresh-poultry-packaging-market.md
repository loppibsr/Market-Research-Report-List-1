<p><ul><li>Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP)</li><li>Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP)</li><li>Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fresh poultry packaging market encompasses several innovative types. Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) replaces air with a protective gas mix to extend shelf life. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly adheres films around the product to minimize air exposure and enhance presentation. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) uses heat to mold a plastic layer tightly around poultry, ensuring freshness. Other packaging methods may include traditional air-tight seals or eco-friendly options, catering to varying consumer preferences and sustainability goals.</p></p>

<p><p>The towel radiators market is divided into two main types: non-thermostatic and thermostatic. Non-thermostatic towel radiators provide consistent heat without temperature regulation, making them simple and cost-effective, suitable for traditional heating setups. In contrast, thermostatic towel radiators feature built-in controls for automatic temperature adjustments, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort. This type is ideal for modern bathrooms, allowing users to maintain a preferred warmth while reducing energy consumption. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences and heating needs.</p></p>

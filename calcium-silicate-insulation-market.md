<p><p>Calcium silicate insulation is categorized into high temperature and mid temperature types, serving distinct industrial needs. High temperature calcium silicate insulation is designed for extreme environments, offering thermal resistance in applications like boilers and furnaces, withstanding temperatures over 1000°F. Conversely, mid temperature calcium silicate insulation functions effectively in moderate heat applications, such as piping and ductwork, providing efficient thermal control and fire resistance up to about 1000°F. Both types enhance energy efficiency and safety in various industrial sectors.</p></p>

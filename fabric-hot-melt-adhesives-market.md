<p><p>The fabric hot-melt adhesives market is segmented into two main types: non-reactive and reactive hot melt adhesives. Non-reactive hot melt adhesives are characterized by their ability to bond fabrics without undergoing a chemical change upon cooling, providing quick assembly and flexibility. In contrast, reactive hot melt adhesives undergo a chemical reaction after application, resulting in stronger, more durable bonds, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing efficiency and performance in fabric bonding processes.</p></p>

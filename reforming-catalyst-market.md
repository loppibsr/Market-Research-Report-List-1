<p><p>The Reforming Catalyst market includes CRR (Continuous Regenerative Reforming) and SRR (Semi-Regenerative Reforming) catalysts, essential for refining processes to produce high-octane gasoline and hydrogen. CRR catalysts are designed for continuous regeneration, enabling efficient, long-term operation with minimal downtime. SRR catalysts, on the other hand, require periodic regeneration, making them suitable for less intensive operations. Both types optimize hydrocarbon conversion and improve fuel quality, catering to diverse refining needs and operational scales in the petroleum industry.</p></p>

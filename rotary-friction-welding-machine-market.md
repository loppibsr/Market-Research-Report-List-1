<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rotary Friction Welding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inertia Rotary Friction Welding</li><li>Direct Drive Rotary Friction Welding</li><li>Hybrid Rotary Friction Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary friction welding machine market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Inertia Rotary Friction Welding** involves a flywheel that stores energy, which is released to generate heat through friction when two workpieces are pressed together. </p><p>2. **Direct Drive Rotary Friction Welding** uses a motor to drive the rotating workpiece directly, allowing for precise control and faster operations. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Rotary Friction Welding** combines features from both inertia and direct drive systems, offering enhanced flexibility and efficiency in welding different materials and geometries.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive electrically driven oil pump market is categorized into two main types: integrated pumps and separate pumps. Integrated pumps are designed as part of the engine assembly, providing a compact and efficient solution that reduces space and weight. In contrast, separate pumps are standalone units that can be installed independently, offering flexibility in design and replacement. Both types aim to enhance engine performance, improve fuel efficiency, and decrease emissions by delivering optimal oil pressure and flow, tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
